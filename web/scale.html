How much land does a datacenter's own power actually need?

Behind-the-meter (BYOP) means the datacenter makes its own electricity on-site with solar + batteries — no grid queue. The catch this page makes visceral: the solar field is enormous next to the buildings it powers, and most of that land isn't even panels — it's the gaps, roads and setbacks between the rows. Everything below is drawn to real scale and uses the exact same sizing math as the siting model.
